About

A comprehensive four-year undergraduate program

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in academics and research in Computer Science and Engineering, tackling challenges for the betterment of society.



Mission
  • Impart professional education aligned with industry needs.
  • Instill ethics, research capabilities, and teamwork.
  • Foster leadership qualities and lifelong learning.
About the Program

This program combines traditional Computer Science with modern Data Science techniques, covering algorithms, data structures, ML, AI, and Big Data.

Practical Learning

Gain hands-on experience through labs, internships, capstone projects, and real-world datasets.
